Arsen Babajanyan

Biography

ARSEN
BABAJANYAN

Munich based Armenian Composer, whose musical path has been started from childhood. Though the main stream of his activity is composition, the range of Arsen's interests are really wide and all the branches of art are involved. This fact is clearly reflected in all his undertakings.

01
Composer

In addition to his main occupation as a composer, he is actively engaged in a social-cultural life of Armenia and activities. He is a Chairman of the Association of Armenian Young Composers and Interpreters since 2010. Since 2016, also Babajanyan is a co-founder and art-director of ARTCONCEPT International Association, which realises art projects and programs all over the world.

The art projects he is authoring aim to promote Armenian culture in international stages, as well as develop Armenian academical art in and outside of Armenia. In scope of those projects the networking and cultural exchange opportunities are established between young performers and international artists.

03
Arts Ambassador

As an art ambassador my goals and activities are always in direction of establishing a sustainable platform for cultural collabaration and development of ways for cultural exchange between artists from different art branches and cultural institutions as well as at activation of international networking for Armenian young artists.

Studying abroad gives him a unique opportunity to understand the advantages of educational and cultural system there, which he is constantly sharing with young generation and just with Armenian artists and art lovers via seminars, forums, conferences, podium discussions, listening sessions, etc.. Those ideas are lying around the core of Arsen's professional path, and they are clearly reflecting in all projects and activities he has authored.

04
Honors & Prizes

During his professional activity Arsen has been awarded several prizes, including 1st prize for the ensemble piece "Traerp" at the 8th pre-art Competition for young composers in Zurich in 2014, the Best Film Music Award at the Żubroffka Festival in Poland, and the DAAD Award for outstanding academic achievement in 2017. In January 2018 he received the 3rd Prize for the 4th String Quartet at the Felix Mendelssohn Bartholdy University Competition in Berlin.

In 2018, he has been awarded with Fridtjof Nansen Gold Commemorative Medal for the activities towards establishing humanitarian principles and condemnation of the Armenian Genocide. In 2019 he was honoured to get 6-month scholarship - residence from Minister of Culture of Bavaria in Paris at Cité Internationale des Arts.

05
Social Activity

Though the main stream of Arsen's activity is composition, the range of his interests are really wide and all the branches of art are involved.

Under this light are his efforts in literary genre, where the scope of Arsen's activity was developed from scientific professional articles and theoretical works to literary compositions, prose and verse. Such as “Thoughts on Harmony” book publication dedicated to the life, music, musical- theoretical and philosophical views of composer, master and RA honored artist Professor Gagik Hovunts, a small book “The philosophy of Garegin Nzhdeh and its actuality” dedicated to Garegin Nzhdeh's works, which was awarded with the second prize by the Republican Party of Armenia, where the first prize was not awarded at all, etc..
