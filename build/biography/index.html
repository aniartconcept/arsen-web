Arsen Babajanyan

Biography

ARSEN
BABAJANYAN

Was born in Yerevan, Armenia in 1986. After taking his bachelor's and masters degrees in homeland, he moved to Warsaw in 2012 and completed a six-month study with the composer Paweł Mykietyn. From 2012 to 2018 Arsen Babajanyan continued his master studies at the University of Music and Performing Arts Munich in the composition class of Prof. Jan Müller-Wieland. Simaltenously he completed Postgraduation in Conservatory for Music and Theater, Graz in subject "Composition and Music Theory" with Prof. Beat Furrer.

01
Composer

Arsen Babajanyan is author of vocal, chamber, symphonic works, as well as theater and cinema music.

03
Honors & Prizes

During his professional activity Arsen Babajanyan has been awarded several prizes, including 1st prize for the ensemble piece "Traerp" at the 8th pre-art Competition for young composers in Zurich in 2014, the Best Film Music Award at the Żubroffka Festival in Poland, and the DAAD Award for outstanding academic achievement in 2017. In January 2018 he received the 3rd Prize for the 4th String Quartet at the Felix Mendelssohn Bartholdy University Competition in Berlin.

In 2018, he has been awarded with Fridtjof Nansen Gold Commemorative Medal for the activities towards establishing humanitarian principles and condemnation of the Armenian Genocide. In 2019 he was honoured to get 6-month scholarship - residence from Minister of Culture of Bavaria in Paris at Cité Internationale des Arts.

04
Arts Ambassador

Arsen Babajanyan is actively engaged in a social-cultural life of Armenia and activities. He is a Chairman of the Association of Armenian Young Composers and Interpreters since 2010. Since 2016, Arsen Babajanyan is also co-founder and art director of ARTCONCEPT International Association, which realises art projects and programs all over the world. The art projects he is authoring aim to promote Armenian culture in international stages, as well as develop Armenian academical art in and outside of Armenia. In scope of those projects the networking and cultural exchange opportunities are established between young performers and international artists.

05
Social Activity

Though the main stream of Arsen Babajanyan's activity is composition, Arsen Babajanyan also has workouts in literary genre, where the scope of his activity was developed from scientific professional articles and theoretical works to literary compositions, prose and verse. Such as “Thoughts on Harmony” book publication dedicated to the life, music, musical- theoretical and philosophical views of composer, master and RA honored artist Professor Gagik Hovunts, a small book “The philosophy of Garegin Nzhdeh and its actuality” dedicated to Garegin Nzhdeh's works, which was awarded with the second prize by the Republican Party of Armenia, where the first prize was not awarded at all, etc..
