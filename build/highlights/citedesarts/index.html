Arsen Babajanyan

Cité internationale des arts

In 2018 Arsen Babajanyan was granted 6-month scholarship-residency in Cité internationale des arts residency in Paris, France by Minister of Culture of Bavaria. During this period Arsen Babajanyan had an opportunity to present his compositions to Parisians with a small portrait-concert.

Arsen Babajanyan’s chamber music works were interpreted by guest musicians Nicky Purser and Varduhi Hakobyan. The compositions were presented for the first time in France, among them, "Sketches for Katharina" for solo piano was an abroad premiere.
