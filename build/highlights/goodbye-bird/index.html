Goodbye, Bird Opera

Composer Arsen Babajanyan, writer Aram Pachyan and animator Eva Muradyan have banded together around the creation of "Goodbye, Bird" chamber opera. Premiere is expected at the end of 2020 in Munich. You can find out more about all the news and upcoming activities by following our page.
